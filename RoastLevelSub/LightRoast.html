Light Roast

Light roast coffees have a light body and pronounced snappy acidity. There is no oil on the surface of the beans. The original flavors of the bean are retained to a greater extent than in darker roasted coffees. Light roasts retain most of the caffeine from the coffee bean. Again, the terminology used is loose, but the following terms are generally assosiated with light roasts:

  • Cinnamon
  • Half City
  • Light City
