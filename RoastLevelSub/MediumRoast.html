Medium Roast

Medium roasted coffees are medium brown in color with more body than light roasts. Medium roasts exhibit a more balanced flavor, aroma and acidity. This roast is often referred to as the American roast because it is generally preferred in the United States. The following are common references reffering to a medium roast:

  • City
  • American
  • Breakfast
